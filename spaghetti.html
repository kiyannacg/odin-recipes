Spaghetti

Describing The Perfect Fried Chicken

Southern Spaghetti Sauce is a thick, flavorful sauce with lots of ground beef, onion, and bell pepper. It has a hint of sweetness to it which really brings out the tomato flavor.

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  2. 1 large onion, diced
  3. 1 green bell pepper, diced
  4. 1 clove garlic, minced
  5. 2 (6-ounce) cans tomato paste
  6. 1 (8-ounce) can tomato sauce
  7. 1 (14 1/2-ounce) can stewed tomatoes
  8. 1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning
  9. 1 tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
  10. 1 tablespoon sugar
  11. 1 teaspoon salt
  12. 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Brown beef in a large heavy bottomed pot. Drain fat.
  2. Add onion and green pepper and saute until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and saute 30 seconds.
  3. Add tomato paste, tomato sauce, stewed tomatoes, seasonings, and water.
  4. Simmer 30 minutes
