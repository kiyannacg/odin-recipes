Fried Chicken

Describing The Perfect Fried Chicken

The perfect fried chicken is crispy on the outside, with a flavorful crust that contrasts and compliments the tender chicken it contains. The starch is deeply browned, allowing the complex flavors created by the Maillard reaction to shine through, without letting anything burn. The meat is perfectly seasoned, and cooked to the point of being tender, but still moist.

Ingredients

  1. 2 lbs chicken wing
  2. 2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  3. 2 Cup Club Soda
  4. 1 1/2 cup buttermilk
  5. 1 Tbsp Salt
  6. 1 Tbsp Italian Seasoning
  7. 1 Tbsp. chicken bouillon
  8. 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  9. 1 Tbsp Onion Powder
  10. 1 Tbsp Ground Black Pepper
  11. 1 Tbsp Paprika
  12. 2 Tbsp Hot Sauce
  13. 1 large egg
  14. Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Clean the chicken with lemon, lime and vinegar
  2. Next place wings in a large bowl
  3. Add a 1/4 cup of red hot sauce
  4. Add 1 1/4 cups of buttermilk and distribute evenly
  5. Cover bowl with saran wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour
  6. Next, drain the buttermilk from the bowl
  7. Add 2 cups of AP flour along with all seasonings
  8. Add the egg and club soda. Incorporate all ingredients
  9. Cover bowl for 30 minutes again
  10. Next remove each from bowl and dredge in flour
  11. Heat cooking oil to 350 degrees and add chicken
  12. Cook for 10-15 minutes
  13. Remove from oil. Place on backing sheet with rack or paper towels to absord oil
