Chicago Hotdog

The Perfect Chicago Hotdog

Starts with a steamed poppy seed bun and an all-beef frankfurter. Then it's topped with yellow mustard, bright green relish, fresh chopped onions, juicy red tomato wedges, a kosher-style pickle spear, a couple of spicy sport peppers and finally, a dash of celery salt.

Ingredients

  1. 6 All beef hot dogs
  2. 6 Poppy seed buns
  3. 2 Tomatoes cut into half moons
  4. 1 White onion diced
  5. 12 Sport peppers
  6. 6 Dill pickle spears
  7. Yellow mustard
  8. 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  9. Bright green relish
  10. Celery salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Lower the heat to a simmer, add the hot dogs and cook for 10 minutes or until heated through. Alternatively, you can steam them in a steamer basket over boiling water until heated through.
  2. Place the hot dogs in the buns and top each with yellow mustard, green relish, chopped onion, two tomato half moons, a dill pickle spear, two sport peppers, and a sprinkle of celery salt.
  3. Resist the urge to reach for the ketchup. Enjoy!
